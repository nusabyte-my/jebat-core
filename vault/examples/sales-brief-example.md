# Sales Brief Example

## Audience
Operations-focused SME founder evaluating NusaByte for dashboard and automation support.

## Sales Stage
Post-discovery, pre-proposal support collateral for internal stakeholder buy-in.

## Offer / Product
Phased browser-based operations dashboard plus ongoing optimization retainer.

## Core Message
NusaByte can reduce operational chaos quickly with a practical first phase, then expand into reporting and automation without forcing a heavy enterprise rollout.

## Proof Points
- phased implementation lowers adoption risk
- reporting turnaround can be improved within the first delivery cycle
- retainer model supports steady optimization instead of large all-at-once scope

## Common Objections
- concern that internal team is too small to support a new system
- fear that the project will become a custom-software money pit
- uncertainty about whether a browser dashboard is enough for operations staff

## Desired Next Step
Equip the founder with a concise one-pager and objection-handling summary before the proposal review.

## Assets Needed
- one-pager
- objection-handling notes
- short capabilities summary

## Roles Needed
- Penggerak Jualan
- Strategi Jenama
- Jurutulis Jualan
- Penulis Cadangan

## Verification
Check that claims align with actual delivery model and that objections are answered without overpromising.
