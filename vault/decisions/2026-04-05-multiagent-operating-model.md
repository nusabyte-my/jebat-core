# Multi-Agent Operating Model For NusaByte

**Date:** 2026-04-05
**Status:** Accepted

## Decision

Expand JEBAT from a narrow engineering crew into a broader multi-agent operating system covering delivery, quality, design, growth, and knowledge work, while keeping one shared Codex core.

## Why

The current workspace covers engineering, security, database, automation, and memory, but NusaByte work regularly crosses product planning, UI/UX, browser implementation, app delivery, SEO, marketing, content production, copywriting, research, and validation.

## Operating Shape

- **Command layer:** Panglima, Hikmat, Agent Dispatch
- **Delivery layer:** Tukang, Tukang Web, Pembina Aplikasi, Bendahara, Syahbandar
- **Quality and risk layer:** Hulubalang, Penyemak
- **Design and growth layer:** Senibina Antara Muka, Pengkarya Kandungan, Jurutulis Jualan, Penjejak Carian, Penggerak Pasaran
- **Knowledge layer:** Pawang

## Workflow

1. Capture objective, stack, constraints, and risk
2. Route to the minimum useful specialist set
3. Investigate in parallel for independent questions
4. Convert findings into a concrete spec
5. Execute by clear ownership
6. Validate with a separate pass when the work is meaningful
7. Consolidate decisions and learnings

## Impact

- routing becomes explicit for growth, design, QA, and cross-layer product work
- shared behavior remains centralized in `skills/_core/CODEX_CORE.md`
- future skill additions should plug into the same precedence and handoff model
