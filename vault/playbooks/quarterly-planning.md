# Quarterly Planning Playbook

Use this playbook for quarterly roadmap planning, retainer reprioritization, growth planning, capacity decisions, and next-quarter operating alignment.

## Trigger

Use when the goal is to set next-quarter priorities based on monthly reviews, current scope, growth opportunities, support load, delivery capacity, and commercial posture.

## Primary Roles

- Panglima
- Strategi Produk
- Penggerak Pasaran
- Penganalisis
- Khidmat Pelanggan
- Penulis Cadangan when renewals, scope changes, or commercial shifts matter
- Relevant delivery leads

## Workflow

1. Gather Inputs
- Collect monthly reviews, open risks, backlog themes, support signals, and current commercial constraints
- Use `vault/templates/quarterly-plan.md` as the planning scaffold

2. Assess Reality
- Separate core commitments, optional bets, renewal-sensitive work, and deferred items
- Identify what the team can realistically execute in the next quarter

3. Prioritize
- Route product direction through Strategi Produk
- Route growth bets through Penggerak Pasaran
- Route support and retention pressure through Khidmat Pelanggan
- Route metric-backed tradeoffs through Penganalisis
- Route renewal or scope implications through Penulis Cadangan

4. Shape The Quarter
- Define top priorities, ownership, success signals, and major constraints
- Clarify what is intentionally not included

5. Consolidate
- Update project memory when quarterly priorities or scope posture changes
- Write a decision file when planning rules or recurring commitments change future behavior

## Deliverables

- quarterly plan
- prioritized themes and initiatives
- risks, exclusions, and dependencies
- success metrics and owners
