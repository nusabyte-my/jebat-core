# Renewal Strategy Playbook

Use this playbook for renewals, upsell planning, scope renegotiation, contract continuation decisions, and end-of-term commercial strategy.

## Trigger

Use when a retainer, contract, or recurring engagement is approaching renewal and the goal is to decide whether to renew as-is, expand, reduce scope, or restructure the commercial model.

## Primary Roles

- Panglima
- Penulis Cadangan
- Penganalisis
- Khidmat Pelanggan
- Strategi Produk or Penggerak Pasaran depending on the dominant workstream
- Penggerak Jualan if outbound collateral or enablement support is needed
- Penyemak if the renewal language creates material commitment risk

## Workflow

1. Gather
- Collect monthly reviews, quarterly plans, support signals, metrics, and scope drift notes
- Use `vault/templates/renewal-brief.md` as the working scaffold

2. Assess Value
- Separate delivered outcomes, unrealized value, recurring pain, and commercial risk
- Identify whether the current structure should renew, expand, reduce, or change format

3. Route
- Commercial framing -> Penulis Cadangan
- Performance evidence -> Penganalisis
- Support and relationship health -> Khidmat Pelanggan
- Product-led scope change -> Strategi Produk
- Growth-led renewal angle -> Penggerak Pasaran
- Sales support assets -> Penggerak Jualan

4. Shape Renewal
- Define recommended renewal posture
- Clarify scope changes, assumptions, exclusions, pricing posture, and next-step asset needs

5. Verify
- Use `vault/checklists/content-copy-verification.md`
- Add `vault/checklists/product-support-verification.md` if scope clarity or delivery boundaries are at risk
- Use Penyemak when the renewal creates meaningful commercial or delivery commitments

6. Consolidate
- Update project memory if the renewal changes scope, cadence, or ownership
- Write a decision file if renewal policy or recurring commercial structure changes future behavior

## Deliverables

- renewal brief
- renewal recommendation
- scope-change summary
- follow-up asset list
- verification summary and residual commercial risk
