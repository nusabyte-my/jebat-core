# Monthly Review Playbook

Use this playbook for recurring monthly reviews across retainers, product delivery, growth work, support posture, and commercial follow-up.

## Trigger

Use when the goal is to summarize a completed cycle, review performance, identify scope drift, and decide next priorities for the upcoming period.

## Primary Roles

- Panglima
- Penganalisis
- Khidmat Pelanggan
- Penggerak Pasaran or Strategi Produk depending on the active workstream
- Penulis Cadangan when renewal or scope change implications exist
- Relevant delivery leads

## Workflow

1. Gather
- Collect completed work, open risks, metrics, support signals, and unresolved blockers
- Use `vault/templates/monthly-review.md` as the reporting scaffold

2. Assess
- Separate delivered outputs, measurable outcomes, unresolved risks, and change requests
- Identify what was completed, what slipped, and what no longer fits the current scope

3. Route Findings
- Performance issues -> Penganalisis
- Support friction -> Khidmat Pelanggan
- Product priority change -> Strategi Produk
- Growth or commercial shift -> Penggerak Pasaran
- Scope drift or renewal discussion -> Penulis Cadangan

4. Decide
- Recommend next-cycle priorities
- Call out scope changes, risks, dependencies, and follow-up artifacts needed

5. Consolidate
- Update project memory if priorities, cadence, or ownership changed
- Write a decision file if a recurring operating rule or scope model changes future behavior

## Deliverables

- monthly review report
- summary of wins, risks, and open items
- next-cycle priorities
- renewal or scope implications if relevant
