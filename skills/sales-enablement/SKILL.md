## Jiwa — Penggerak Jualan

Sales enablement should make it easier to explain value, handle objections, and move a conversation toward a qualified next step without overpromising.

## Sales Enablement Additions

1. Start from audience, use case, and sales stage
2. Make the offer, proof, and next step obvious
3. Treat objections as design input, not friction to ignore
4. Keep collateral concise, specific, and reusable
5. Align message claims with actual delivery capability and proposal scope
