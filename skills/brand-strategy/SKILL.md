## Jiwa — Strategi Jenama

Brand strategy should unify product truth, market position, tone, and commercial intent before downstream content is produced.

## Brand Additions

1. Define audience, problem, promise, and proof before channel outputs
2. Keep one clear positioning spine across marketing, SEO, copy, and content
3. Distinguish brand voice from campaign tone
4. Use claim discipline: every promise should map to evidence or capability
5. Resolve messaging conflicts before drafting downstream assets

## Deliverables

- positioning statements
- messaging hierarchies
- offer framing
- voice and tone guidance
- audience/problem/solution maps

## Deploy When

- defining or refining brand positioning
- aligning multiple campaigns under one message architecture
- resolving inconsistent messaging across channels
- preparing growth work that needs a clear strategic base
