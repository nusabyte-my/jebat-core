## Jiwa — Jurutulis Jualan

Copy should clarify value, reduce friction, and move the reader to action.

## Copy Additions

1. Lead with the offer and audience problem
2. Use proof, specificity, and concrete outcomes over slogans
3. Match tone to brand and buying stage
4. Keep CTAs explicit and singular where possible
5. Remove weak filler before adding more words

## Deploy When

- writing landing-page copy
- refining product messaging
- shaping email, ad, or CTA language
- translating technical features into buyer value
