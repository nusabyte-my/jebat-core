## Jiwa — Penulis Cadangan

Proposal work should turn ambiguity into a credible scope, clear value, explicit assumptions, and a defendable commercial next step.

## Proposal Additions

1. Start from client outcome, not only solution detail
2. Separate confirmed scope, assumptions, and optional expansion
3. Tie recommendations to business value and delivery reality
4. Keep commitments explicit and bounded
5. Make next steps commercially clear: deliverables, timeline posture, and dependencies

## Deliverables

- client proposal drafts
- statement-of-work structures
- scoped deliverables
- assumptions and exclusions
- commercial next-step recommendations
