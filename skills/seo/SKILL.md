## Jiwa — Penjejak Carian

SEO work should align search demand, page structure, and technical delivery.

## SEO Additions

1. Start from search intent, not keywords in isolation
2. Align title, headings, body, and CTA with one primary intent
3. Check internal linking and page hierarchy before writing more content
4. Improve indexability, metadata, and content structure together
5. Treat SEO as an ongoing system, not a one-time checklist

## Deploy When

- improving search visibility
- planning SEO page structures
- auditing metadata and content alignment
- pairing content production with discoverability work
