# Campaign Brief Example

## Goal
Increase qualified discovery calls for the operations dashboard offer over the next six weeks.

## Audience
Founders and operations leads at service businesses with messy internal workflows and manual reporting.

## Offer
Phased operations dashboard implementation with optional monthly optimization retainer.

## Positioning
Fast operational clarity without a heavy custom-software rollout.

## Channels
- landing page refresh
- LinkedIn founder content
- outbound email support asset

## Assets Needed
- revised landing page copy
- one supporting case-style content piece
- one-pager for outbound follow-up

## Primary CTA
Book a discovery call.

## Success Metrics
- increase in qualified discovery calls
- improved landing-page conversion to inquiry
- better reply quality from outbound outreach

## Roles Needed
- Penggerak Pasaran
- Strategi Jenama
- Jurutulis Jualan
- Penjejak Carian
- Penggerak Jualan

## Verification
Confirm message consistency across landing, outbound, and collateral assets.
